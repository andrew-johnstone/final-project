This site started as little more than a meme that popped off the top of my head when I was trying to decide what I wanted my final project to focus on. While the idea for this site may have been off the cuff, my fascination and appreciation for octopuses has been more of a permanent fixture in my brain for the past few years. While it's obvious from first glance that octopuses are truly unique and magnificent animals, I never would have anticipated the capacity that these creatures possess to solve complex problems, play, and form bonds with their caretakers. As humans become more conscious of what it means to be conscious, I truly believe that we must begin to better respect the unique and incredible minds of all the self-aware creatures and attempt to be better neighbors to them so that we may continue to grow and learn from each other in our own ways.
