Octopuses are fascinating creatures which you would be forgiven for mistaking as alien lifeforms at first glance. Sporting 8 flexible arms, a shimmering, camoflaging skin, and a self-made supply of defensive ink, they are truly unlike any other creature on this planet. Perhaps the most remarkable features of octopuses (that's the correct plural by the way!) are their large, complex, and conscious brains. Every octopus has its own personality for you to explore and discover, so please stick around and get to know these alien minds!

Meet an Octopus

Meet Tatoosh, a giant Pacific octopus named after the Washingtonian island where he was first found! Giant Pacific octopuses are the largest members of their octopus family and are arguably the smartest known invertabrates in the world! These mollusks are immensely strong, capable of moving loads of up to 700 pounds in weight with their 8 powerful and independent arms. These illusionists of the intertidal zone live an average of only 3-5 years. Sadly, Tatoosh passed away in 2021, but his work as an ambassador of his species touched the hearts of many and allowed us to increase our understanding of these fascinating minds.
